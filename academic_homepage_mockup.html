I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in Political Science at Columbia University.

My research examines leadership and legislative politics using AI-driven methods and multimodal datasets.

My dissertation project, The Cultural Logic of Accountability, explores how leadership ideals shape political accountability across contexts.

I have been invited to present this work at MPSA 2026 and APSA 2026 panel sessions.
